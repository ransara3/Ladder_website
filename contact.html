Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about your stay.

What amenities are included?

Our room includes air conditioning, comfortable bedding, a private bathroom with hot/cold water, and daily housekeeping.

🚗

How do I get to the resort?

We are located in Anuradhapura. You can reach us by bus, train, or private vehicle. We can arrange airport transfers on request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments in LKR and USD. Please contact us in advance for other payment arrangements.
