Explore Anuradhapura

Discover ancient temples, sacred ruins, and natural wonders — all within easy reach of our resort.

Sacred & Historical Sites

Anuradhapura is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with a rich heritage spanning over 2,500 years.

🏛️

Ruwanwelisaya Stupa

5 km from resort

One of the tallest ancient monuments in the world, this sacred white stupa was built by King Dutugemunu in 140 BC. A stunning example of ancient Sri Lankan engineering and devotion.

🌳

Sri Maha Bodhi

4 km from resort

The oldest historically documented tree in the world, planted in 288 BC. Grown from a branch of the original Bodhi tree under which Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

⛩️

Jetavanaramaya

6 km from resort

Once the tallest brick structure in the world, this massive stupa stands at over 70 metres. A remarkable feat of ancient architecture dating back to the 3rd century.

🏊

Kuttam Pokuna (Twin Ponds)

6 km from resort

Two magnificent ancient bathing pools showcasing the sophisticated hydraulic engineering of ancient Sri Lanka. Beautifully preserved with ornate stone carvings.

🗿

Abhayagiri Monastery

7 km from resort

A vast monastic complex that was once home to 5,000 monks. Explore the ancient ruins, moonstone carvings, and the towering dagoba of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

🦌

Wilpattu National Park

30 km from resort

One of Sri Lanka's premier wildlife parks, home to leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and diverse birdlife. A must-visit for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Visitor Tips

Make the most of your Anuradhapura visit with these helpful tips.

👗

Dress Code

When visiting temples and sacred sites, please wear clothing that covers your shoulders and knees. Remove footwear and hats before entering sacred areas.

🕐

Best Time to Visit

Early morning is the best time to explore the ruins, when it's cooler and less crowded. The dry season (May to September) offers the best weather for sightseeing.

🚗

Getting Around

The ancient city sites are spread across a wide area. We recommend hiring a tuk-tuk or bicycle to comfortably explore all the major attractions.
