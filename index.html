Our Story

A Legacy of
Refined Hospitality

Perched amidst lush highlands and panoramic vistas, Ladder Resort has been a haven for discerning travellers since 1999. Our philosophy is simple: every guest deserves an experience that lifts them above the everyday.

Spanning over 50 pristine acres, the resort seamlessly blends contemporary luxury with the raw beauty of nature. From our award-winning restaurant to our tranquil infinity pool overlooking the valley, every corner of Ladder invites you to unwind and reconnect.
