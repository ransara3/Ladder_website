Explore Anuradhapura

Discover ancient temples, sacred ruins, and natural beauty — all within easy reach of our resort.

🏛️

Ruwanwelisaya Stupa

5 km from resort

One of the most sacred Buddhist stupas in Sri Lanka, this magnificent white dome is a must-visit landmark.

🌳

Sri Maha Bodhi

4 km from resort

The oldest historically documented tree in the world, planted in 288 BC from a branch of the original Bodhi tree.

🏊

Kuttam Pokuna

6 km from resort

The Twin Ponds — stunning ancient bathing pools showcasing remarkable hydraulic engineering from centuries past.
